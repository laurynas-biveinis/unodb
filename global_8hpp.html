◆ UNODB_DETAIL_HEADER_NOINLINE

#define UNODB_DETAIL_HEADER_NOINLINE   UNODB_DETAIL_NOINLINE inline

Define in a header a function that should not be inlined.

This is a pair of two seemingly conflicting intentions: "noinline" and "inline". However, only the "noinline" has anything to do with inlining. The "inline" has nothing to do with inlining and is required for functions declared in headers.
