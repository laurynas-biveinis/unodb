Detailed Description

Lexically scoped guard to pause heap allocation tracking and faulting for this thread.

To be used for specific allocations that are outside of the tested code, such as constructing test diagnostic messages.

Note
Do not instantiate directly: use UNODB_DETAIL_PAUSE_HEAP_TRACKING_GUARD() instead.
