Detailed Description

A helper class used to expose a consistent snapshot of the unodb::detail::key_prefix to the iterator for use in tracking the data on the iterator's stack.

This method exposes a key_view over its internal data. We can't do that directly with the unodb::detail::key_prefix due to (a) thread safety; and (b) the key_view being a non-owned view. So the data are atomically copied into this structure and it can expose the key_view over those data.
